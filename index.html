Our Facilities

Private Dining Room Options

Golf's Steak House & Seafood offers a variety of secluded dining areas to accommodate groups ranging from 6 to 60 guests, ensuring both privacy and comfort for your event.

Solarium Room

Accommodates up to 18 guests. Easily accessible from the main lobby with partial Grand River views.

Back Room

Suitable for 30 to 48 guests. Features frosted windows for privacy.

Inside Room

Fits 24 to 40 guests. Mobility-friendly and located near the salad bar.

Front Room

Hosts 20 to 60 guests. Largest dining room area, divisible for smaller groups.

Note: A gratuity of 18% is added to groups of 10 guests and larger.

For more information or to make a reservation, please contact Golf's Steak House & Seafood at (519) 579-4050.

Banquet Facilities

Our elegant banquet halls provide the perfect setting for weddings, birthdays, corporate events, and so much more. Whether you're celebrating a milestone, hosting a professional gathering, or planning a romantic reception, our versatile spaces can be tailored to suit your needs. From intimate gatherings to grand celebrations, let us help you create cherished memories that will last a lifetime.

Ballroom

Elegant and spacious, the Ballroom includes a private bar, washrooms, entrance, and coat room. Max capacity: 220 (with dancing).

Royal Room

Warm and inviting, the Royal Room is perfect for intimate receptions. Includes private bar, washrooms, coat room, and entrance. Max capacity: 120 (with dancing).
